Wipro

Wipro Limited is a leading global information technology, consulting and business process services company. We harness the power of cognitive computing, hyper-automation, robotics, cloud, analytics and other emerging technologies to help our clients adapt to the digital world and make them successful.Wipro Limited is a multinational corporation based in India that provides information technology, consulting, and business process services. It was founded in 1945 by M.H. Hasham Premji as Western India Palm Refined Oil Limited, which later abbreviated to Wipro. Over the decades, it transitioned from a manufacturer of vegetable and refined oils into a diversified conglomerate with interests in various sectors. However, Wipro's major growth trajectory came with its focus on IT services and consulting. Today, it's one of the largest IT services companies in India and competes globally with other major players like Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Infosys, and HCL Technologies. Wipro offers a wide range of services including software application development, maintenance, systems integration, and IT consulting.

Our Reach

Wipro has a global presence with offices in over 60 countries and serving clients across various industries.Wipro's reach extends across the globe, with a significant presence in numerous countries and serving clients from various industries. Here are some key aspects of Wipro's reach: Global Offices: Wipro has offices in over 60 countries worldwide, enabling it to serve clients globally and provide localized support. Client Base: Wipro caters to a diverse range of clients spanning multiple industries such as banking, financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, technology, and telecommunications. Its clientele includes Fortune 500 companies as well as smaller enterprises. Service Offerings: Wipro offers a comprehensive suite of services including software application development, systems integration, IT consulting, business process outsourcing, digital transformation, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and analytics among others. This broad range of services allows Wipro to address various client needs across different sectors. Partnerships and Alliances: Wipro has strategic partnerships and alliances with leading technology providers such as Microsoft, SAP, Oracle, IBM, and Salesforce. These partnerships enable Wipro to leverage cutting-edge technologies and deliver innovative solutions to its clients. Delivery Centers: Wipro operates delivery centers across the world, including in India, the United States, Europe, Asia Pacific, and the Middle East. These delivery centers ensure proximity to clients and provide scalable resources for project delivery. Innovation Hubs: Wipro has established innovation hubs and digital labs in key locations to drive research, development, and innovation in emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, Internet of Things (IoT), blockchain, and augmented reality. Sustainability Initiatives: Wipro is committed to sustainability and corporate social responsibility. It actively engages in environmental conservation, community development, and social welfare initiatives in the regions where it operates, contributing to its positive impact on society.
